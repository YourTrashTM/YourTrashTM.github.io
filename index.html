WELCOME TO COREBreach

CoreBreach is a regional infrastructure partner providing network reliability, signal maintenance and long-range communications support for communities across the Pacific Northwest. Since 1974 we have worked alongside local governments, utilities and emergency services to keep critical connections online in difficult terrain.

From ridge-top relay towers to low-power remote monitoring sites, our teams design and maintain systems that keep information moving when weather, distance or geography make conventional coverage unreliable. Our focus is simple: stable uptime, clear signal paths and dependable support for the organizations that rely on us.

What We Do

  • Design and maintenance of rural and mountainous relay networks.
  • Support for public safety and emergency response communications.
  • Long-range signal testing and site surveys for new build-outs.
  • Monitoring of infrastructure performance for partner municipalities.

CoreBreach currently supports service corridors in and around Hollowpine Ridge, the interior Cascadia region and selected coastal townships. To learn more about how our systems integrate with your existing infrastructure, visit the Solutions section.
