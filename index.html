ABOUT COREBreach

CoreBreach is a federally contracted infrastructure solutions provider specializing in network reliability, signal maintenance, and long-range communications across rural and mountainous regions. Founded in 1974, the organization has played a critical role in supporting public safety networks and environmental monitoring operations in the Pacific Northwest.

Our installations are trusted by local governments, utility cooperatives, and emergency response providers to maintain uptime in even the most geologically complex terrain. All operations are conducted in full compliance with regional and federal regulatory standards.

OUR SERVICES

  • Installation and upkeep of fiber-optic and coaxial cabling
  • Signal relay and tower network design for remote terrain
  • Diagnostics for analog and digital communication systems
  • Environmental telemetry routing for weather and wildlife sensors
  • Secure field infrastructure consulting for federal projects
